Tom Holland

Your friendly neighbourhood Spider-Man

Here is a list of some of Tom Holland's Achievements

  • 2012 – Starred in The Impossible, gaining international recognition.
  • 2015 – Cast as Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
  • 2016 – Made his MCU debut in Captain America: Civil War.
  • 2017 – Headlined his first solo MCU film, Spider-Man: Homecoming.
  • 2019 – Starred in Spider-Man: Far From Home, which became a box office hit.
  • 2020 – Appeared in Netflix’s The Devil All the Time, showing a darker acting range.
  • 2021 – Released Spider-Man: No Way Home, one of the highest-grossing films of all time.
  • 2022 – Played the lead in Uncharted, expanding his action-hero resume.
  • 2023 – Starred in and executive produced the series The Crowded Room on Apple TV+.
